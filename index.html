Biography

I am a Research Engineer at Institute of High Performance Computing, Singapore under A*STAR (AI Programme). I have varied interests including but not limited to programming, machine learning techniques and artificial intelligence. Specifically, I find working on imparting human capabilities of understanding language to machines interesting, and hence have been involved in several research projects at the nexus of natural language processing and machine learning.
Currently, I am more focus on natural language processing tasks such as machine comprehension, neural sequence labeling, commonsense reasoning, and also any other attractive tasks involved machine learning techniques.

Projects

*
All Deep Learning Natural Language Processing Image Processing
Punctuation Restoration and Sentence Boundary Detection

A bidirectional recurrent neural network with attention mechanism and CRF for punctuation restoration and sentence boundary detection in unsegmented text.

Neural Sequence Labeling

A fast and accurate deep learning based approach for common sequence labeling tasks such as Part Of Speech (POS) Tagging, Chunking, Named Entity Recognition (NER) and etc.

WordNet Troponymy and Action Hierarchy Extraction

Action hierarchy and Result-Manner relation extraction for commonsense reasoning and natural language processing.

PrimeNet: Human-inspired Framework for Commonsense Knowledge Representation and Reasoning

A human-inspired framework for commonsense knowledge representation and reasoning.

Removing Backscatter to Enhance the Visibility of Underwater Object

A novel and effective method based on underwater optical model for underwater object visibility enhancement.

Image Enhancement based on Retinex Theory and Dual-tree Complex Wavelet Transform

An improved and effective method for image enhancement based on retinex theory and dual-tree complex wavelet transform techniques.
